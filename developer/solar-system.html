Solar System

The Solar System project was all about the simulation of our system. While researching you notice how every image you had of the solar system is so warped and scaled to fit into a picture. If you take the real scale of the planets and the real distance everything is so small that you see nothing if everything is in frame. This just gives you a scale of how big our universe actually is. And it looks pretty.
