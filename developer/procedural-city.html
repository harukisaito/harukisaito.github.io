Procedural City

This is a city that can be procedurally created and was the foundation of another project about visualization of geographical data. I modeled some buildings in Unity with the ProBuilder Package and then spawned them inside a "block". These blocks were then spawned on a bigger grid to make up this city. The size of the blocks and the grid can be manipulated freely to make the city as big as one wants to or the computer can handle.
